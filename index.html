Xaime Rivas Rey
Industrial and Electrical Engineer

My name is Xaime Rivas Rey. I was born in A Coruña, in the north-west of Spain in 1992. Since 2014, I have had a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering Technologies from the "Escuela Politécnica de Ferrol" which is part of the "Universidade da Coruña". In October of 2016, I finished a double-degree Master’s program with the EAGLES scholarship, which earned me both the Spanish Master of Science in Industrial Engineering from the "Polytechnic University of Madrid" and the Master of Science in Electrical Engineering from Drexel University. Currently, I'm a PhD student at Drexel University in the Drexel Wireless System laboratory, where I serve as a Research and Teaching assistant.

My main research interests focus on Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
